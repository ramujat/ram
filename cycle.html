A baby walker is a popular infant mobility device designed to help babies learn to walk and explore their surroundings. It typically consists of a frame with wheels and a padded seat where the baby can sit and move around. Baby walkers are equipped with a variety of entertaining features and toys to keep the baby engaged and encourage motor skill development.

Baby Walker with Wheels:

A baby walker with wheels is a mobile device that allows a baby to move around, supported by a frame with wheels. The wheels enable the baby to glide across the floor and explore their environment in a safe and supervised manner. The seat is usually adjustable in height to accommodate the baby's growth and ensure a comfortable and secure fit.
